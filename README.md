Arogya Mitra is a smart healthcare web application developed during a 24-hour inter-collegiate national level hackathon held at Kristu Jayanti College, where our four-member team secured first prize. The application functions as a personal medical companion that helps users track daily health metrics, manage medications, access AI-powered health guidance, and perform essential medical actions through a clean and intuitive dashboard. The project was conceptualized, designed, and built collaboratively under strict time constraints with a strong focus on real-world healthcare usability and impact. 


 **Live Demo:** https://v0-arogya-mitra-blond.vercel.app/

---


## Project Overview

Arogya Mitra serves as a **personal health dashboard and wellness assistant** for everyday users. Upon logging in, users are presented with a health snapshot that includes:

- Daily wellness metrics
- Medication tracking
- AI-based health assistance
- Appointment and emergency utilities

The goal is to **simplify health management** and make preventive care more accessible.

This application is ideal for users who want to:
- Monitor daily health habits
- Manage medicines efficiently
- Get quick AI-based health guidance
- Organize appointments and emergency information
